Email Editor
Changes saved here update your Script Properties — the actual email text your leads receive. Use {{firstName}} anywhere in the body and it will be replaced with the lead's first name. Email 5 has extra fields for price and CTA links.
Open & Click Data
Open tracking works via a 1×1 pixel in the HTML email — fires when the email client loads images. Gmail prompts users before loading images, so ~40–60% open rate is realistic.
CTA click tracking (Email 5 only) — all links route through your Apps Script redirect before landing on your sales page. Every click is logged.
UTM parameters are auto-appended to all Email 5 links: utm_source=email_sequence&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=dsm_email_5 — visible in Google Analytics.
Time-on-email is not technically trackable in standard email — only web pages can measure it. The open count proxy is the best available signal.
